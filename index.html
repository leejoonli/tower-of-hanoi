Tower of Hanoi

Tower of Hanoi rules

  1. Click on a disk to select it.
  2. Click on the tower space to move your selected disk.
  3. Only one disk may be moved at a time.
  4. Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack or on an empty space.
  5. No disk may be placed on top of a disk that is smaller than it.
